What does it take to pass IELTS?

The following tips will help you succeed

First of all, find out your current level of English by doing this placement test. After that, make sure that your level corresponds to the target band you need in IELTS. Then, make a schedule to devote each day to a particular skill. Practice active listening to podcasts on academic topics and colloquial English. Read academic journals and collect ideas and vocabulary. Finally, put all this knowledge into speaking and writing practices.
